Frequently Asked Questions

Can Non Himachali Buy?

Non-Himachali do not require any special permission to buy or lease constructed property, flat, etc. Anyone who is a citizen of India can freely buy any constructed property in the state, However there is a specific regulation for Registering a property outside Municipal Limits known as the Section 118 of the H.P. Tenancy and Land. Reforms Act, 1972.

How To Check a Property is Legit?

There are Doctuments Like Title Deed, Channel Document,Encumbrance Certificate, TCP Approval, Jmabandi, Approved plan to Assist in Verification of a Viable Real Estate Investment.
